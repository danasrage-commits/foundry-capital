About Foundry

Built to Be Nigeria's Foundational Institution

Foundry Capital Africa was conceived from a simple but infuriating observation: Nigeria has everything required to produce world-class companies — talent, market size, entrepreneurial energy, and consumer pain points so acute that solutions generate immediate revenue.

What Nigeria lacks is not ideas. It lacks the infrastructure around the ideas. Foundry builds that infrastructure.

"Africa's constraint is never the idea. It is the operational, financial, and network infrastructure around the idea. We build that infrastructure."

The Process

From Raw Idea to
Investable Company

Every Foundry portfolio company passes through three phases. Each phase is milestone-driven, operationally supported, and backed by Foundry's corporate and government partner network.

0
Phase 0 · Weeks 1–12

Validation Grant Period

Before any equity changes hands, Foundry awards a validation grant of ₦500,000 to ₦2,000,000 to test a core hypothesis. No pitch deck required. No polished financial model. We want to know: can this founder identify a real problem and learn from the market in 90 days?

One-page hypothesis document submitted at intake
Bi-weekly check-in with Foundry Scout
One warm introduction per month to relevant expert
90-day Validation Report triggers Build-in-Residence decision
1
Phase 1 · Months 1–6

Build-in-Residence

The heart of the Foundry model. Six months of intensive co-building where operational infrastructure is embedded directly into the founding team. Foundry takes 8–15% equity in exchange. By graduation, the company has traction, governance, and often a paying corporate customer already in place.

Dedicated Venture Partner — 10hrs/week minimum, paid role
Shared CFO builds financial model and accounting infrastructure
CTO pool review for technical architecture decisions
Legal templates, ESOP setup, regulatory filings handled
Corporate Strategic Partner introduction in Month 2
Peer Cohort: 8–10 companies building together, learning laterally
2
Phase 2 · Month 7 Onwards

Seed Investment & Growth

Post-graduation, Foundry deploys $50,000–$500,000 in seed capital from Fund I. The company now has a Foundry board observer, access to follow-on capital, and warm introductions to Series A funds. Foundry's Portfolio Dashboard tracks every company's KPIs in real time so problems are caught early.

$50K–$500K seed capital deployed post-milestones
40% of Fund I reserved for follow-on in best performers
Series A warm introductions: TLcom, Partech Africa, Novastar
Board observer seat, quarterly governance support
→
Exit

Five Exit Pathways

Foundry builds toward exit from Day 1. The Corporate Strategic Partner network creates natural trade sale buyers. The LP network creates Series A secondary sale opportunities. Pan-African regional expansion creates acquisition by East and Southern African buyers. The largest outliers target NGX listing.

Trade sale to Corporate Strategic Partners (40–50% of exits)
Secondary sale to growth-stage funds at Series A/B
Pan-African regional acquisition (East/South African buyers)
NGX Growth Board IPO for largest portfolio companies

What We Look For

We Don't Require Revenue.
We Require Obsession.

🎯
A Real Problem
Something you understand intimately because you've lived it, researched it exhaustively, or spent time with the people experiencing it. Not a trend you read about.
🇳🇬
Nigerian or West African Focus
The problem must affect Nigerian or West African consumers or businesses. You can be diaspora-based — we support founders building from abroad.
💡
A Plausible Business Model
Not a complete financial model. Just a credible hypothesis for how money eventually gets made. Who pays, why, and roughly how much.
🏗️
Willingness to Co-Build
Foundry embeds deeply. This means sharing information, accepting operational support, and engaging genuinely with your Venture Partner. We're partners, not spectators.
⚡
One of Our Four Sectors
Agritech, Healthtech, Fintech, or Energy. We may expand sectors in Cohort 2 — but Cohort 1 focuses where Foundry's corporate network is deepest.
👥
At Least One Technical Co-Founder
Or a credible plan to recruit one in Month 1. We provide a CTO pool for architecture decisions, but we don't build your product for you.
